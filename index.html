    The Town

    Come and explore the small town in the heart of Namaqualand popular for it's annual spring flower display. This usually happens between the months of August to September (depending on rainfall). Situated just outside of town is the Letterklip provincial Heritage site. This unique rock formations that is fortified by dry rock stone walling is a popular tourist attraction.
